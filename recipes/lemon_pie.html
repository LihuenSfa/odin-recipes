Lemon Pie

Description:

A sweet and acid dessert that will be the main attraction of your tea parties.

Ingredients:

For the pie crust:

  • 250gr 0000 flour
  • 150gr unsalted butter
  • 100gr powdered sugar
  • 1 lemon peel
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1 egg

Lemon mousse:

  • 60gr corn starch
  • 200gr sugar
  • 90gr egg yolks
  • 200cc lemon juice
  • 2 lemon peels
  • 500cc water
  • 50gr unsalted butter

Steps for the pie crust:

  1. Mix the pie ingredients, except the egg, until you get a sand like consistency
  2. Add the egg and mix until you get the cake dough
  3. Reserve in the fridge until the dough is completely hard
  4. Use the heat of your hands and a rolling pin to give form to the pie crust
  5. Bake in a pre heated oven for 20 minutes

Steps for the lemon mousse:

  1. Mix the corn starch with 100gr of sugar
  2. Add the yolks and a little bit of the lemon juice
  3. Whisk vigorously to avoid lumps
  4. Add the rest of the lemon juice and the lemon peels
  5. In a pot boil the water with the rest of the sugar
  6. Mix half of the boiled water with our lemon mixture, don't forget to keep beating
  7. Now add the lemon mixture to the pot and continue to whisk until everything thickens
  8. At least add the butter and whisk until you get an homogenous mix
  9. Put the mousse on top of the baked pie crust, cover with adhesive film
  10. Reserve in the fridge overnight

You can eat it like this, but I suggest you that you top your basic lemon pie with some delicious italian meringue.
