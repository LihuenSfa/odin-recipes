Perfect rice

Description:

This rice is rich, buttery, and so comforting. It is cooked perfectly, not mushy or sticky, and has a nice savory flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 ½ cups of rice
  • A pitch of salt
  • Chicken stock or water

Steps:

  1. Coat each grain of rice in the butter before boiling
  2. Season with salt and add any aromatics that you want
  3. The longer the rice is toasted in the butter, the deeper the flavor will be.
  4. You can stir the rice once when you add the liquid and once more when it comes to a boil, but that’s it! Cover the pot and don’t peek until it’s done.
