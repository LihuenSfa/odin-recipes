Italian meringue

Description:

The very famous italian meringue, a perfect topping for your desserts, to mix in a buttercream or just to eat alone.

Ingredients:

  • 3 egg whites
  • 220gr sugar
  • 70cc water

Steps:

  1. Beat the egg whites until they double in size
  2. While beating put the water and sugar to boil in a pot
  3. When the bubbles are big retire the pot from the fire and drip the content into the bowl with the whites
  4. Don't stop whisking until you reach the desired consistency
